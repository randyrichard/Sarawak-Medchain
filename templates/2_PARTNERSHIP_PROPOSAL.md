## The rules that protect BOTH of us

These will be in the Founders' Agreement. Read them now so nothing surprises you later:

1. **Vesting.** If you leave the company before 12 months, you get zero equity. After 12 months you get 25%; then it releases monthly over 3 more years. This means you can't leave in month 6 and take 10% of the company with you — but I also can't fire you unfairly and take back what you've earned.

2. **IP assignment.** Everything you create for MedChain — marketing copy, brand assets, customer lists — belongs to the company, not to you personally. If you leave, they stay.

3. **Non-compete (12 months post-departure).** You won't join or start a competing blockchain healthcare business in Malaysia for 12 months after leaving. Everywhere else in the world is fair game.

4. **Confidentiality.** Anything you learn about MedChain (technical, customer, financial) stays confidential. Even after you leave. This is standard, not personal.

5. **Termination clauses.** Either of us can walk away. But there are process rules — 30-day notice, no ghosting. Your vested equity stays yours (subject to vesting schedule at the time of departure).

6. **Sdn Bhd registration trigger.** If we haven't registered Sdn Bhd within 18 months, either of us can walk away with no penalty. This protects you if I never get around to the Sdn Bhd step.

7. **Founder dispute resolution.** If we disagree on something major, we sit down for 3 days of honest conversation. If we still can't agree, we bring in a mutual friend (mediator). If still no resolution, we go to Kuching High Court. But we do the human process first.
