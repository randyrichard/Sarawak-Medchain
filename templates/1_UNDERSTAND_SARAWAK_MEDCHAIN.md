## What Sarawak MedChain is (plain language)

Every doctor in Malaysia issues **medical certificates (MC)** — the paper you show your boss when you take sick leave. Right now, these MCs are:

- **On paper** — easily photocopied and forged
- **Impossible to verify** — your boss can't tell if it's real
- **Not tracked** — nobody knows how many are issued or by whom

This costs Malaysia an estimated **RM 1.5–3 billion per year** in fraud, sick leave abuse, and insurance claim fraud (based on Ministry of Health healthcare spend and global NHCAA fraud benchmarks).

**Sarawak MedChain fixes this** by putting medical certificates on a blockchain:

- Doctor issues an MC → it's encrypted and gets a unique cryptographic fingerprint on a public blockchain
- Patient controls who can read it — via a phone wallet OR a simple 6-digit code (like an OTP)
- Employer scans the QR code → verifies in 5 seconds if it's real
- Everything is logged, auditable, and impossible to fake
