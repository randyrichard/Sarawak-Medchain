## Tone notes

- **Acknowledge the gap honestly** — "it's been a while" — but don't apologize for it. Apologies invite "no problem, let's catch up later" rather than action.
- **Lead with proof** — the live demo link in the second paragraph does more work than any paragraph of selling.
- **Make the ask concrete** — "support letter to SGH" is a specific document, not vibes.
- **Double-purpose the Dr Steve question** — asking for his details gives Ayappa an easy, low-effort reply (just a name and a department) that reopens the dialogue even if the support letter takes longer.
- **No urgency / no pressure** — "this week or next" signals confidence, not desperation.
