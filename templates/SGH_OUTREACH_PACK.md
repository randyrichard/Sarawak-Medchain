## The 3 people to try to reach (in order of ideal outcome)

### Priority 1: Pengarah Hospital (Hospital Director)

- **Role:** Top decision-maker at SGH
- **Best contact route:** Via the Director's PA / Setiausaha (personal assistant)
- **How to find:** Call SGH main line +60 82-276 666 → ask for "Pejabat Pengarah" (Director's Office) → ask for the PA's email
- **Realistic outcome:** PA schedules you a 10–15 min meeting or refers you to the right department head

### Priority 2: Head of IT / Digital Health / Medical Informatics

- **Role:** Owns the technical decision for anything digital/e-MC
- **Best contact route:** LinkedIn search "Sarawak General Hospital IT" or "Hospital Umum Sarawak digital"
- **Realistic outcome:** They understand the tech immediately, can champion internally

### Priority 3: Head of Outpatient / Emergency Department

- **Role:** Highest MC-issuance volume in the hospital
- **Best contact route:** LinkedIn or through mutual connections
- **Realistic outcome:** They see the pain point of MC volume, become an internal advocate
