# FOUNDERS' AGREEMENT — DRAFT TEMPLATE

## ⚠️ IMPORTANT NOTICE — READ BEFORE USING

**This is a TEMPLATE prepared for negotiation between founders. It is NOT a substitute for legal advice.**

**Before either party signs this document:**

1. **Both parties must engage a Malaysian-qualified lawyer** to review and adapt this document to your specific situation.
2. Typical cost: RM 200–800 for a founders' agreement review by a lawyer in Kuching or KL. This is money well spent — it prevents six-figure disputes later.
3. This template covers *common* provisions but may not cover *your* specific needs. A lawyer will help identify what's missing.
4. Under Malaysian law, some provisions in this document (particularly around a not-yet-existing Sdn Bhd) may require adaptation to be fully enforceable.

**Nothing in this document is legal advice. Consult a lawyer.**

## 3. ENTITY, EQUITY & VESTING

### 3.1 Current entity status

The Parties acknowledge that the Company is presently registered as an Enterprise (sole proprietorship). Sole proprietorships under Malaysian law do not issue equity shares.

### 3.2 Commitment to Sdn Bhd conversion

The Founding Party commits to incorporating a private limited company (Sdn Bhd) under the Companies Act 2016 within **eighteen (18) months** of the Effective Date, or within thirty (30) days of the first signed paid customer contract, whichever is earlier.

### 3.3 Equity allocation upon Sdn Bhd incorporation

Upon incorporation of the Sdn Bhd, the initial share capital shall be allocated as follows:

- **Randy Richard:** [___]% (default: 90%, if Tier A / 94% if Tier B / 97% if Tier C)
- **[Friend's Name]:** [___]% (default: 10% if Tier A / 6% if Tier B / 3% if Tier C)

The Joining Party's shares shall be subject to the vesting schedule in Section 3.4.

### 3.4 Vesting schedule (Joining Party)

The Joining Party's equity vests on the following schedule from the Vesting Commencement Date:

- **First 12 months (the "Cliff"):** ZERO shares vested. If the Joining Party ceases involvement for any reason prior to the 12-month anniversary, they receive no equity.
- **12-month anniversary:** 25% of allocated equity vests immediately.
- **Months 13 through 48:** the remaining 75% vests in equal monthly increments (approximately 2.08% per month) over 36 months.
- **48-month anniversary:** 100% vested.

*For Tier C (Sweat Equity Advisor), vesting shall be 2 years total with a 6-month cliff instead.*

### 3.5 Milestone-based equity bonuses (Tiers B & C only)

Additional equity may be granted subject to the following performance milestones:

- **Tier B:** Additional 2% granted upon Company reaching 3 paying customers.
- **Tier C:** 1% additional per paying customer personally closed by Joining Party, up to a maximum of 3%.

Milestone equity vests immediately upon grant (no additional cliff).

### 3.6 Acceleration on change of control

If the Company is acquired or merges with another entity ("Change of Control"), 50% of the Joining Party's then-unvested equity shall accelerate and vest immediately.

### 3.7 Interim equivalent (before Sdn Bhd)

Between the Effective Date and the date of Sdn Bhd incorporation, the Joining Party has no legal shareholding but has an enforceable contractual right to the share allocation defined in Sections 3.3 and 3.4 upon incorporation.

## 6. NON-COMPETE

### 6.1 During the term

Neither Party shall directly or indirectly engage in, own, or be employed by any business that competes with the Company anywhere in Malaysia during the term of this Agreement.

### 6.2 Post-departure (Joining Party only)

For a period of twelve (12) months after the Joining Party ceases involvement with the Company, the Joining Party shall not:

- Engage in any blockchain-based medical certificate or e-MC business in Malaysia
- Solicit any Company customer, prospect, or employee
- Use any Company Confidential Information for a competing purpose

This restriction is limited to Malaysia. Activities outside Malaysia are unrestricted.

---

## 7. TERMINATION

### 7.1 Voluntary departure

Either Party may terminate their involvement with 30 days' written notice to the other Party. The Joining Party's vested equity as of the departure date remains theirs, subject to Section 7.4.

### 7.2 Termination for cause

Either Party may terminate the other's involvement immediately for cause, including:

- Material breach of this Agreement not cured within 14 days of written notice
- Fraud, dishonesty, or gross misconduct
- Conviction of a criminal offence involving moral turpitude
- Unauthorised disclosure of Confidential Information

In case of termination for cause of the Joining Party, all unvested equity is forfeited and vested equity is subject to Company buy-back at fair market value.

### 7.3 Termination if Sdn Bhd not incorporated

If Sdn Bhd is not incorporated within eighteen (18) months of the Effective Date, either Party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' notice, with no penalty and no ongoing obligations except confidentiality and non-compete.

### 7.4 Company buy-back right

Upon departure of the Joining Party, the Company (or Founding Party as successor-in-interest) has the right, but not the obligation, to buy back vested shares at:

- **Fair market value** if departure is voluntary or without cause
- **Original allocation cost (par value)** if departure is for cause per Section 7.2

Fair market value shall be determined by mutual agreement or, failing agreement within 30 days, by an independent valuer appointed by both Parties.
